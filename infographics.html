Art is the quintessential manifestation of human artifice, wherein the creator pushes the envelope of conventional perception to transmute raw aesthetic intuition into a profound, transcendent experience. Studying it like a discipline, which is what I tried to do while I was still graduating; so, this is just unlettered patois, and so, definitely even feel more exasperating to the nth degree, moreover because it's me who's talking, whose character is also congruent to all this.

I approached this discipline of art by understanding its core aspects, like form, style, cultural essence and traditional knowledge, placing emphasis not on the processes of creation, interpretation and transmission. By employing diverse media, techniques, and documentation tools, I developed proficiency in visual and sensory analysis. Ultimately, by linking aesthetic, cultural and scientific dimensions, I understood the patterns that render this study both reflective and practical.
